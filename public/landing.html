Features

Everything You Need to Preserve Memories

From elegant flip-books to custom themes, Memoria gives you the tools to create albums that last a lifetime.

Flip-Book Experience

Beautiful animated page-turning effect that feels like flipping through a real photo album. Smooth, premium, and delightful.

Custom Themes

Choose from Wedding, Birthday, Anniversary, and more. Each theme comes with matched colors, fonts, and border styles.

Rich Media Galleries

Upload up to 4 photos or videos per page. Choose from square, rounded, circle, or polaroid frame styles.

One-Link Sharing

Share your album with anyone via a single link — no account required. Perfect for gifting and preserving memories together.

Rich Text Editor

Add poems, captions, and stories alongside your photos. Bold, italic, headings — full formatting control every page.

Global Border Styles

Apply a consistent border — floral, classic frame, or modern minimalist — across every page for a cohesive look.
